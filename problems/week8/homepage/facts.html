Facts!

Everyone loves facts, so here's a couple:

  1. Sharks' Ancient Evolution - Sharks have been around for over 400 million years, predating dinosaurs by more than 200 million years
  2. Bananas Are Berries - Botanically speaking, bananas are berries, but strawberries aren't! This is due to the way their seeds and fruit develop
  3. Octopus Hearts - Octopuses have three hearts: two pump blood to the gills, and one pumps it to the rest of the body. When they swim, the heart that delivers blood to the body stops beating
  4. The Eiffel Tower Grows - In summer, the Eiffel Tower can grow up to 6 inches due to the expansion of its iron structure in the heat
  5. Honey Never Spoils - Archaeologists have found pots of honey in ancient Egyptian tombs that are over 3,000 years old and still perfectly edible!
